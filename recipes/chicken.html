Chicken Recipe

Description

This reipe has been hand-crafted by Kevin himself to bring out the most flavor.

This is a meal Kevin survived off of during his formative years.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Baking Tray

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees Farenheight
  2. Place raw unfrozen chicken on to baking tray (use foil for easy clean up)
  3. Drizzle EVOO over the chicken and ensure it is evenly coated above and below
  4. Liberaly season chicken with salt and pepper
