Potato Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Small Golden Potatoes
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Baking Tray
  • Boiling Pot

Steps

  1. Wash potatoes by hand
  2. Place washed potatoes into boiling pot for 10-15 minutes
  3. Once potatoes are soft enough to poke with a fork, remove from heat
  4. Remove potatoes from water
  5. Place potatoes on baking tray (lined with foil for easy clean up)
  6. Drizzle EVOO onto potatoes for an even coat
  7. Then liberally season the potatoes with salt and pepper
  8. Smash potatoes with a fork, be careful not to smash completely
  9. Add anothe drizzle of EVOO for extra crispy
  10. Place in oven for 20 at 420 degrees F
