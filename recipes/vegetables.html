KG's Vegetable Recipe

Description

These are the veggies that rocky ate during his training camp to fight Drago

If these veggies don't put a pep in your step then you're already dead

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Bell Peppers
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Water
  • Pot for steaming

Steps

  1. Wash vegetables
  2. Place pot with 1/4 cup of water on heat
  3. Place vegetables in pot and cover with lid
  4. After 5 minutes season vegetables with a pinch of salt and pepper
  5. Mix vegetables in seasoning then let steam for 5 more minutes
  6. Remove from heat and serve
